Privacy Policy for Sheridan Navigator

Last updated: 16 April 2024

Welcome to the Sheridan Navigator app ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users ("you", "your", "user") of our campus navigation app. This privacy policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your information.

1. Information Collection

When you register on the Sheridan Navigator app, we collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored using encryption)

While you use our app, we also collect:

  • Geolocation data to determine your current campus location for navigation purposes.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Email for registration and login authentication.
  • Password to secure your account access (note that we never store passwords in plain text).
  • Geolocation data to provide you with accurate campus navigation services.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored in Firebase Realtime Database, which includes robust security measures to protect your information. We use industry-standard encryption to safeguard the data transmitted between the app and our servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access and update your personal information within the Sheridan Navigator app at any time. Should you wish to request the deletion of your data, please contact us at the information provided below.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
